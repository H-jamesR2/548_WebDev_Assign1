Pigs:

Pigs are any of the animals in the 'Sus' genus within

the even-toed ungulate family 'Suidae'.

Pigs, domestic pigs and their ancestor, the Eurasian

wild boar (Sus scrofa), along with its other species..

originated from the Eurasian and African continents,

ranging from Europe to the Pacific islands.

Typical pigs have a large head with a long snout

which is a very acute sense organ used

to dig into soil to find food.

Fun Fact: The pig, among the most populous large

mammals in the world, are very social and intellgient.
